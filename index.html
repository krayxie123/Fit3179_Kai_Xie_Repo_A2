VCE School Results Across Victoria

VCE results have been a great big deal in Australia

Victoria is also known as the education state. But just how well educated is the entire state? Is there disparity between regions and inequality? And how has this lead to higher education?

Median VCE Study Scores by Locality

Understanding VCE Scores:

• 30 is the median score for all states for a subject

• 50 is the maximum mark achievable

• Scores above 40 are considered excellent

• Median scores vary significantly by school and region

Geospatial Median Score Correlations:
As we increase the VCE score threshold , we can observe how the median scores for each suburb. Higher thresholds reveal that metropolitan areas and regions closer to the city tend to maintain higher median scores, while regional and remote areas show more significant drops in performance. This pattern demonstrates the correlation between geographic proximity to metropolitan centers and educational outcomes, highlighting the resource disparities that impact VCE performance across Victoria.
