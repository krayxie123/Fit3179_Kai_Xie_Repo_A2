Regional VCE Performance Analysis

Distribution patterns and average scores across Victorian regions reveal significant educational disparities. South Eastern Victoria is well known to have some of the best schools in Victoria and is the most educated region, consistently excelling in VCE performance. The density plot shows the distribution patterns of scores across regions, while the bar chart reveals the average performance levels.

The distributions show that larger metropolitan regions tend to have more diverse score ranges, with both high-performing and struggling schools, while smaller regional areas often show more concentrated performance patterns. This reflects the complex relationship between geographic location, socioeconomic factors, and educational outcomes across Victoria.
